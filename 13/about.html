• About Minions Restaurant

    Minions Restaurant is a family owned restaurant, established by one of the greatest contemporary artists Mona Bano
    She brought other arts such as painting and fashion in her dishes which makes every dish unique.
    Indeed it’s hard to eat a piece of art but you can’t resist the taste.
    Great atmosphere of this restaurant doesn’t let you leave and you have to know that every week you are going to see a new theme in Minions.
